About the Destination

A Soulful Escape
on the Arabian Sea

Gokarna is a small, sacred coastal town nestled in the Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka, India. Renowned for the ancient Mahabaleshwar Temple, it draws pilgrims and beach lovers alike — a rare fusion of spirituality and serenity.

Unlike the buzzing beaches of Goa, Gokarna's shores remain refreshingly unhurried. Om Beach, Kudle Beach, Half Moon Beach, and Paradise Beach each carry their own distinct character — from lively cafés to pristine silence only the waves can fill.

Trek between beaches along dramatic clifftop trails, watch fishermen cast their nets at dawn, or simply sit beneath a coconut palm as the sun dips into the sea — Gokarna rewards every kind of traveller.

3-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival & Om Beach

  • 🚆 Arrive at Gokarna Road Station
  • 🏨 Check in at a beachside guesthouse
  • 🛕 Visit the ancient Mahabaleshwar Temple
  • 🌅 Sunset stroll along Om Beach
  • 🍛 Dinner at a beach-shack café
Day 02

Beach Hopping Trek

  • 🌄 Sunrise yoga on Kudle Beach
  • 🥾 Trek from Om → Half Moon → Paradise Beach
  • 🤿 Swim & snorkel at Half Moon Beach
  • 🥥 Coconut water breaks along the trail
  • 🔥 Bonfire evening at Paradise Beach
Day 03

Leisure & Departure

  • ☕ Slow breakfast overlooking the sea
  • 🛍️ Shop for souvenirs at the local market
  • 📸 Final photography walk
